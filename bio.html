for work, collaboration, or anything fun, please do not hesitate to contact me :)
My interest in design focuses on the concept of “interaction” and “intersection”, especially in-between human and machine, objects and empathy. Coming from a mixed background between computation, humanities and Industrial Design, I have a passion for blending the unlikelies together, and the majority of my work rests at the points where opposite disciplinaries intersect with each other.
