CITIBIKE STEEL ANGELS

This was a project I was involved in as a designer and maker while working at Tomorrow Lab. It is the design and production of something called the Steel Angel Key, a special reward for Citi Bike members that volunteer to redistribute bikes across the city. They provide an invaluable service that benefits other users, as well as the environment. I was involved in every phase of the process, from design to manufacturing.
