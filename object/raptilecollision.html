RISD CE Global Program : Nuremberg - Toy Design Project No.2 : Animal Themed Nerf Blaster.

This was a three week project focusing on designing an original animal-themed blaster for ages 6+up that has a compelling role-play story behind the play. The requirement was that the final blaster design may shota foam dart or ballToy needs to have a fun play pattern supported by an interesting role-playing story. Toy may include other props such as targetsFeatures should make sense with theme. Name of toy should sound fun, describe the play and be memorable. Toy should look fun and be fun to play with!
