WIKIPEDIA REDESIGN

This project focuses on redesigning the Wikipedia web interface for cleaner visuals, better usability and greater content authenticity.

Background Context

Wikipedia has long been one of the biggest online encyclopedia on the web, yet not only the design is outdated, its reputation has gone down, from a platform based in large collections of reputable sources, to a place filled with easily manipulated and unreliable texts. This redesign focuses on looking into the context and history of Wikipedia, and through research and design iterations provide a new look and interaction framework that transforms the user experience of this platform.
